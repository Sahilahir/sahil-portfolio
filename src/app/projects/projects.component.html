ECOTRACK

2017 - 2019

For two years, the research was done as part of the Design Engineering course. Design Engineering's aim was to recognise real-world challenges and offer solutions for addressing or removing them. The idea was to make Bicycles available for hire in the city. Bicycles ought to be used as both a means of transportation or a piece of exercise equipment. With proper authentication, anybody can use these bicycles for their own reason. We used JAVA NETBEANS to create the desktop framework for allocating and deallocating bikes and keeping track of records, as well as MYSQL for backend database help. With a commitment to the System's programming, I took on tasks like Database Connectivity and Interface Design. It was a significant undertaking. The project was important to me because it was my first project as project leader.



GO DRIVE

2019 - 2020

It was an Industry Defined Project for the final year (IDP). We finished it with an internship at KNOXPO Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a prestigious tech solutions firm. We worked on Angular, Firebase, and Android Studio for this project. The aim of the project was to develop a digital forum for the motor driving school and its customers. The aim of "GO DRIVE" is to fill a gap in the market for a digital channel for driving schools. It enables someone who wants to learn to drive to find their favourite driving school and time slot, and then go to drive. The other features of “GO DRIVE” assist the driving school in managing trainers, clients, and accessing former teacher and customer details. It also provides the school with analytics in a graphical format. We used Angular for web application creation because we wanted to build a single-page web application that was simple to maintain. We have used Bootstrap, a framework for creating appealing, sensitive, and interactive web applications. The backend was driven by Firebase, and the mobile app was developed with Android Studio. The project was also under my leadership. I was in responsibility of the web application and the Firebase integration. With GITHUB, we even learnt about version control.



WEB APPLICATION FOR DIAMOND INDUSTRY

2021

I was hired as an intern at Tradinget. It is one of the most promising companies which provides an online B2B and B2C marketplace for the Diamond and Jewellery Industry. The project was about the automation and digitalization of the whole company's procedure for diamonds. The app was made with PHP and MySQL. I have worked on both Frontend and Backend. I have contributed to all the modules, but most of my contribution was in Accounting, Backup, and Report module. For the first time, I worked under someone's leadership, and this experience was a must for me as I got an understanding of how to work under pressure under someone's supervision. It was a great experience, and the project was very informative as well.
